MEDICAL COMMUNICATIONS

The Medical Communications division of ERS offers the full range of pre/post-launch promotional programs and communication services for pharmaceutical clients. ERS offers an advantage to clients by employing expert account management teams who are skilled in combining scientific proficiency, marketing know-how and skillful client servicing expertise.

The following are some of the promotional services offered by the ERS Medical Communications division:

  • Senior-Level Project Management
  • Market Insight Research & Analysis
  • Speaker Training Programs / Speaker Bureau Management
  • Advisory Board Meetings
  • Health Care Professional Webcasts & Teleconferences
  • Strategic Publication Planning (Articles, Abstracts, Posters, Newsletters, Monographs)
  • Slide Deck Creation & Content Development
  • Key Opinion Leader Research & Analysis
  • Meeting Recruitment & Logistics Management
  • Advertising (Advertorials, Journal Ads)
  • Formulary Kits, Sell Sheets, Brochures, Visual Aids and other print collateral
  • Targeted Web and Interactive Programs (see ERS eLearning Division)
