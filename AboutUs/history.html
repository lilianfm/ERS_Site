COMPANY HISTORY

In 1994, Gregg Terry launched a new endeavor in pharmaceutical sales training with the conception of Educational Resource Systems, Inc. (ERS). To support his vision, he assembled the best talent in the areas of medical writing, medical illustration, instructional design, web-based programming, project management and meeting planning. Since then, the ERS team has capitalized on his vision and successfully developed over 1,700 web-based, CD-ROM, and print-based programs, while assisted in launching over 63 different products in virtually every therapeutic area.

In 2009, ERS expanded by creating a Medical Communications division with the collective proven experience in managing and executing speaker training meetings, advisory board meetings, and a variety of promotional projects. The Medical Communications team has perfected the art of client servicing, developing and maintaining key opinion leader (KOL) relationships, and integrating medical writing and graphic design into promotional materials. This division of ERS is proud to have both local and international clients who have products in diverse therapeutic areas.

In 2007, ERS incorporated an eLearning department into the Sales Training division. The eLearning team focuses on creating exciting, interactive adult-learning systems, utilizing programs such as HTML5 and Articulate

Storyline to develop a selection of animations and customized applications. eLearning projects can range from module page turners, to sophisticated mobile apps and interactive booth activities, including augmented reality.

With a strong foundation in pharmaceutical sales training and medical communications developed over 20 years, ERS continues to build upon years of experience, while raising the bar as a cutting edge leader in the ever changing pharmaceutical marketplace. ERS’ new office is located in Building Six of the Galleria in Red Bank, NJ. To read about the history of the Galleria and other businesses located here, please visit www.thegalleriaredbank.com.
