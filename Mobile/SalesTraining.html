SALES TRAINING

With over two decades of experience, the Sales Training division of ERS has been exceeding client expectations with its unique blend of extensive therapeutic knowledge, unmatched creativity, and account management expertise.

The following are some of the Live Sales Training services offered by the ERS Sales Training division:

  • New product or indication, POA meetings
  • Comprehensive training covering disease state application through market dynamics, messaging introduction/updates and final certification
  • Turn-key, highly interactive customized workshop designs (DVD-driven, Video, and/or Talking PowerPoint)
  • Customized and graphically rich supporting workshop materials (posters, game boards, patient case files, worksheets, etc.)
  • Designs built on sound principles of adult learning
  • Knowledge assessments using computer-based games and/or audience response mechanisms
  • Script writing service to support live broadcast format
  • Sales simulations utilizing corporate staff or actual customers
  • Experienced in partnering with numerous production companies and meeting planning groups to coordinate needed logistics for training
  • ERS staff available for on-site meeting and event support

eLearning

Due to new developments and advances in technology, new methods of learning have become available. Not only are these electronically based learning systems a new way to engage the learner, but they also allow for training a larger amount of people while reducing travel and venue costs.

ERS offers a variety of systems that can be adapted to the needs of each individual client:

  • Simple “Page Turners”
    Easy-to-use web-based learning module to be read in a page turning format
  • On-line systems developed in Articulate
    Highly versatile programs that allows for easy translation and usage in different countries
  • Flash animated DVDs/on-line systems
    Gives a visually enhanced and interactive learning experience; DVDs are portable and can be used at any location
  • Interactive Learning systems for LMS application
    On-line availability makes this a highly accessible learning forum; it can include integrated testing and polling
